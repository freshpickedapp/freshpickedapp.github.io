About FreshPicked

Community Connections

FreshPicked connects neighbors who grow their own fruits and vegetables with those looking for fresh, organic produce.

Sustainability First

Our app fosters local connections and helps reduce food waste while promoting sustainability and eco-friendly living.

Empowering Growth

By joining FreshPicked, you contribute to a community that values local gardening and supports healthy living practices.

Key Features

AI-Powered Postings

To save the user’s time and allow them to post their produce within seconds, AI can generate your entire post.

Local Pickup

Find fresh produce nearby and pick up items directly from your neighbors.

Garden Showcase

Show off your garden setup and inspire others with your gardening skills.

Community Interaction

Share gardening tips, recipes, and join local events in our social section.

Smart Garden Helper

FreshPicked’s tool uses deep learning to analyze growth patterns, offering personalized harvest timelines so sellers know exactly when their produce is ready for sale.

Buying & Trading

Buy, sell, or trade produce with secure payment options like Apple Pay.

Sustainability Focus

Support local, eco-friendly gardening and reduce emissions by buying produce nearby.

Tailored Recipes

Our app suggests recipes based on produce in the user’s FreshPicked cart, helping them make the most of their fresh ingredients.

Benefits

  • Reduces food waste by connecting those with surplus produce to those who need it.
  • Offers the freshest, organic options right from your neighbors, ensuring top quality.
  • Encourages local gardening and entrepreneurship.
  • Builds a supportive community around healthy living and gardening.
  • Sustainable and eco-friendly options for your kitchen.
  • Tastes better and fresher than store-bought produce.
