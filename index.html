About FreshPicked

FreshPicked connects neighbors who grow their own fruits and vegetables with those looking for fresh, organic produce. Our app fosters local connections and helps reduce food waste while promoting sustainability.

Key Features

Easy Listings

Post your homegrown produce for free or for sale with photos and descriptions to attract buyers.

Local Pickup

Find fresh produce nearby and pick up items directly from your neighbors.

Garden Showcase

Show off your garden setup and inspire others with your gardening skills.

Community Interaction

Share gardening tips, recipes, and join local events in our social section.

Buying & Trading

Buy, sell, or trade produce with secure payment options like Apple Pay.

Sustainability Focus

Support local, eco-friendly gardening and reduce emissions by buying produce nearby.

Benefits

  • Reduces food waste by connecting those with surplus produce to those who need it.
  • Offers the freshest, organic options right from your neighbors, ensuring top quality.
  • Encourages local gardening and entrepreneurship.
  • Builds a supportive community around healthy living and gardening.
  • Sustainable and eco-friendly options for your kitchen.
  • Tastes better and fresher than store-bought produce.
